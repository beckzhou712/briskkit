Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-28

This Privacy Policy applies to the following browser extensions published by Briskkit: Design X-Ray, Tab Suspender (TabSnooze), TimeTrack, Multi-Clipboard, Glance (link preview), and Save Anywhere.

Our principles

  • We do not operate servers that receive your personal data.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or any third party.
  • Except where explicitly stated below (Save Anywhere, which uploads files to the cloud account you connect), all data stays on your own device.
  • We do not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising code.

Per-extension data practices

Design X-Ray

Collects nothing. It runs locally inside the tab you are viewing, and only when you activate it (toolbar click or keyboard shortcut). It does not store or transmit any data.

Tab Suspender (TabSnooze)

Stores only your own settings (such as the inactivity timeout and your per-site whitelist) locally via the browser's storage. It does not read page contents and transmits nothing.

TimeTrack

Records website domains and duration visited locally, stored in browser storage and never uploaded. It does not record full URLs, page contents, form data, or browsing history beyond domain and duration.

Multi-Clipboard

To provide a clipboard history, Multi-Clipboard stores your recently copied text locally in the browser's storage. This stays on your device and is never uploaded.

Glance (link preview)

Stores only your preferences (such as the activation modifier key and the preview window size) locally. Previews load directly in your browser; we do not collect, store, or transmit the pages you preview.

Save Anywhere

Save Anywhere lets you upload a link or image to your own Dropbox and/or Google Drive.

Authentication: You connect your account using the provider's official OAuth flow. We never see or store your password.

Data transmission: When you right-click an item and choose to save it, the extension sends only that specific link or image, plus your access token, directly to Dropbox's or Google's API.

Google Drive scope: The extension uses the drive.file scope, meaning it can only access files it creates; it cannot see the rest of your Drive.

Browsing data: It does not collect your browsing activity or page contents.

Data deletion

Uninstalling an extension removes its locally stored data. Extensions with history features and Save Anywhere provide in-app deletion options.
